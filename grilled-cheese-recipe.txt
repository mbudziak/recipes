Ingredients:
 - 2 slices sandwich bread
 - 2 slices of your favorite cheese
 - Butter
 
Directions:
 1. Heat a non-stick frying pan to medium heat.
 2. Spread button on one side of one slice of bread.
 3. Place buttered bread butter side down on frying pan
 4. Add slices of cheese on top side of bread that is on the frying pan.
 5. Place second slice of bread on top of cheese and butter it.
 6. Wait until bread on frying pan is golden brown then flip sandwich over.
 7. Fry second side of bread until golden brown then remove from pan and place on serving plate.
 8. Enjoy sandwich alone or with a cup of creamy tomato soup.
